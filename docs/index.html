SleepWindow

Plan bedtime, wake time, naps, and caffeine around 90-minute sleep cycles.

What it does

  • Pick a wake time → see bedtimes that end a full sleep cycle.
  • Tap "if I fall asleep now" → see wake times that end a full cycle.
  • Plan power, short, or full-cycle naps.
  • See a conservative caffeine cutoff for your target bedtime.
  • Simple local reminders. No cloud. No account. No analytics.
